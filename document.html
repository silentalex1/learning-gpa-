Welcome to Learning GPA

Your all-in-one helper for school. This guide will help you understand how to use the website effectively to improve your grades.

Getting Started

Simply navigate to the subject you need help with using the buttons on the main page. You can solve math problems, get AI assistance, and save your notes.

Math Tools

Our math solver is designed for precision. Here is how to use the different modes:

  • Linear Equation: Enter formulas like y = 2x + 1. We will graph it and find the intercepts.
  • Intercepts: If you know where the line crosses the axes, enter them (e.g., x-intercept 2, y-intercept 3).
  • Geometry: Enter two coordinate points like (1, 2) and (4, 6) to find distance, midpoint, and slope.
  • Triangles: Enter two angles to find the third interior angle or the exterior angle.

AI Helper

We use advanced AI to explain concepts. The AI can help with:

  • Breaking down complex math problems.
  • Checking grammar for English essays.
  • Explaining historical events.
  • clarifying scientific concepts.

Note: The AI will strictly stick to educational topics.

Note Taking

You can save your study notes directly in the browser.

  1. Log in using the "Login" button.
  2. Click "Your Notes" to open the editor.
  3. Type your title and content. You can even paste images or text from other sites.
  4. Click "Save Your Note".
  5. Right-click a note tab to rename it.
